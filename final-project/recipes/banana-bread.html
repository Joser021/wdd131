Banana Bread Recipe

In a large mixing bowl, mash 3 ripe bananas with a fork until smooth.
Add 2 eggs, 1/2 cup of vegetable oil (or melted butter), 3/4 cup of sugar, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional), and whisk everything together until well combined.
In a separate bowl, mix 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour with 1 teaspoon of baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda, a pinch of salt, and a bit of ground cinnamon if you like.

Let the bread cool in the pan for about 10â€“15 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing and serving.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) vegetable oil (or melted butter)
  • 3/4 cup (150 g) sugar (you can reduce to 1/2 cup for a less sweet version)
  • 1 1/2 cups (180 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • Ground cinnamon to taste (optional)
  • Chopped nuts or chocolate chips (optional)
